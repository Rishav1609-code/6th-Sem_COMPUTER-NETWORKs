## 5. Data Flow Modes (⭐⭐⭐⭐)

### Must Know

### Simplex

```text
One Direction Only
```

---

### Half Duplex

```text
Both Directions
But One At A Time
```

Example:

Walkie-Talkie

---

### Full Duplex

```text
Both Directions
Simultaneously
```

Example:

Mobile Phone Call
